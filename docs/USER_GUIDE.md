# Huong Dan Su Dung CloudCollab

## 1) App nay dung de lam gi?
CloudCollab la app quan ly cong viec va tai lieu nhom theo phong cach all-in-one:
- Quan ly workspace theo team/project.
- Viet va luu tai lieu theo page/block.
- Gan task cho thanh vien, theo doi tien do, han chot.
- Dong bo lich Google Calendar/Meet de hop va theo doi agenda.
- Nhan thong bao su kien quan trong va nhan tin trao doi trong inbox.

Ung dung phu hop cho:
- Nhom do an, startup nho, team van hanh, team san pham.
- Nhom can vua docs, vua tasks, vua lich hop trong cung mot noi.

## 2) Bat dau nhanh (lan dau)
1. Dang ky tai khoan.
2. Tao workspace moi.
3. Moi thanh vien bang email va phan role.
4. Tao page tong quan (muc tieu, scope, deadline).
5. Tao task, gan assignee, due date, priority.
6. Ket noi Google de tao/sync lich hop va su kien.

## 3) Cac role trong workspace
- owner: toan quyen quan tri, role, thanh vien, cau hinh.
- editor: tao/sua noi dung, task, moi thanh vien (khong gan owner).
- viewer: xem noi dung, task duoc giao co the cap nhat trang thai/priority/due date cua task do.

## 4) Luong su dung chinh
### A. Quan ly workspace
1. Vao Workspaces.
2. Bam "Create workspace".
3. Vao workspace detail de moi thanh vien.
4. Nguoi duoc moi vao Workspaces se thay incoming invitation de accept/refuse.

### B. Lam viec voi tai lieu
1. Mo workspace.
2. Tao page va block noi dung.
3. Comment, mention email (@user@example.com) de thong bao nguoi lien quan.
4. Dung search de tim nhanh title/content.

### C. Quan ly task
1. Vao Tasks (trong workspace hoac My Tasks).
2. Tao task: title, assignee, due date, priority.
3. Theo doi theo list/board va cap nhat status.
4. Neu task bi blocked/done/qua han -> inbox se co notification quan trong.

### D. Lich hop Google
1. Vao Profile ket noi Google.
2. Tu Tasks page, tao meeting/event.
3. Zoom lich de de nhin, click event de xem popup chi tiet.
4. Event co linked task va assignee de bam vao lam viec tiep.

### E. Inbox thong bao va nhan tin
1. Vao Inbox.
2. Tab Notifications: nhan mention, assignment, deadline, overdue, invitation, status updates.
3. Tab Messages: chon thanh vien chung workspace de nhan tin 1-1.
4. Tin nhan co thread, unread count, va danh dau da doc tu dong khi mo thread.

## 5) Quen mat khau
1. O man hinh login bam "Quen mat khau?".
2. Nhap email da dang ky.
3. Mo link reset password.
4. Dat mat khau moi va dang nhap lai.

Luu y:
- Moi refresh token cu se bi thu hoi sau khi reset mat khau.
- Moi session dang nhap cu se can dang nhap lai.

## 6) Thung rac
- Logic thung rac cua pages dang theo co che soft-delete.
- Khuyen nghi thao tac: xoa nham -> vao khu vuc lien quan de khoi phuc som, tranh xoa day cap.

## 7) Meo de app de dung hon
- Tao template page cho moi workspace (Kickoff, Weekly Plan, Risks).
- Dat quy uoc ten task: [TEAM] [MUC TIEU] [HAN CHOT].
- Uu tien gan assignee ro rang, khong de task "vo chu".
- Moi ngay vao Inbox truoc, xu ly unread truoc khi vao task detail.

## 8) Cac loi thuong gap
- Khong thay workspace: kiem tra da accept invitation chua.
- Khong gui duoc message: can chung workspace voi nguoi nhan.
- Khong thay su kien Google: kiem tra ket noi Google va quyen Calendar.
- Link reset het han: yeu cau forgot password lai.

## 9) Quy trinh de xuat cho team (daily)
1. Sang: mo Inbox -> xu ly notification quan trong.
2. Trua: cap nhat status task dang lam.
3. Chieu: check lich hop/ngay mai tren calendar.
4. Cuoi ngay: tong ket tren page team + danh dau task done.
